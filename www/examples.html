Idaho Association of Business Legislative Priorities

IAB supports policy efforts to create a climate that allows small business to grow and fosters new business formation across Idaho. IAB recognizes this must include a wide range of polices including, but not limited to, equitable and efficient tax policy, improved workforce development, education reform and less regulation. Following are the current top priority issues for IAB during the 2016 Legislative cycle.

Tax Reforms

IAB supports tax reforms, including, but not limited to, creating a more efficient and equitable environment for small businesses to prosper. This includes moving to a more competitive tax rate for small business while still maintaining the revenue needed for the proper role of state government.

Health Care

IAB supports affordable, quality health care through innovation, choice and competition in the marketplace. IAB opposes burdensome mandates on small employers and supports efforts to reduce the complexity and litigation of our healthcare system.

Education / Workforce Development

Idaho’s biggest asset is our workforce and future workforce. IAB supports a “K through Career” Education system, that results in an educated and prepared workforce providing our citizens with economic opportunity and our business with the best talent possible. Business in Idaho has stake in the quality of our entire education system, from elementary schools through our universities and vocational training programs. IAB supports strengthening our education system through innovation, choice and cost-effective investment.
