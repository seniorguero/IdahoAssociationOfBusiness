Idaho Association of Business Legislative Priorities

What size companies does the IAB represent?

We have a broad array of companies that we work with. However, we are best set up to work with small to medium size companies. We know the sort of problems that you face and are ready to help you to be successful.

What are the benefits of membership in IAB?

IAB’s mission is to: “Create and maintain an economic climate that allows our members, their employee’s, and all Idaho citizens to prosper.” IAB members believe that private sector job growth is the key to our state’s prosperity, and drives opportunity for all Idahoans. For those who share our dedication to private business, adding ACTION to their principles is the primary driver for membership.

What opportunities does membership in IAB provide?

Join IAB committees that shape TEST policymaking.

  • Network with other business leaders who share concerns about policy issues that affect their companies.
  • Meet elected state policymakers and discuss business concerns with them.
  • Help elect pro-business leaders to statewide office and the Idaho Legislature.
